About DAVIS

The power and data beneath your feet.

DAVIS Malaysia is a specialist manufacturer of floor box systems, wiring accessories, and power distribution units. Their products are installed in raised-floor offices, trading floors, data centres, and convention halls where clean, accessible power and data connections are essential.

CRI distributes DAVIS floor box systems in the Philippines, supplying projects that require reliable underfloor power and data access without compromising floor aesthetics.
