About HOCHIKI

Over a century of fire detection excellence.

HOCHIKI Corporation is one of the world's most respected fire alarm manufacturers, with products deployed in over 100 countries. Their ESP (Enhanced System Protocol) addressable platform is renowned for reliability and backward compatibility across generations of systems.

CRI is an authorized distributor of HOCHIKI fire detection products in the Philippines, bringing Japanese engineering quality to local installations across hotels, condominiums, and industrial facilities.
