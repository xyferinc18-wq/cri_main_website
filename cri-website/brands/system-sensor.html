About System Sensor

Detection and notification trusted worldwide.

System Sensor, a Honeywell brand, is one of the most recognized names in fire detection and life-safety notification. Their product range includes photoelectric and ionization smoke detectors, heat detectors, multi-criteria sensors, duct smoke detectors, and the iconic SpectrAlert Advance horn/strobe series.

CRI supplies and installs System Sensor detectors and notification appliances as part of integrated fire alarm systems across commercial and industrial projects in the Philippines.

Key Products

Sensors and notification that define the industry standard.

4W-B Photoelectric Smoke Detector

2-wire and 4-wire photoelectric smoke detector with drift compensation — compatible with all major conventional fire alarm panels.

i3 Series Multi-Criteria Detector

Intelligent multi-criteria detector combining smoke, heat, and CO sensing to maximize detection accuracy and minimize false alarms.
