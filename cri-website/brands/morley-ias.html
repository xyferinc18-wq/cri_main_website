About Morley-IAS

British fire engineering for demanding installations.

Morley-IAS Fire Systems is a British fire alarm brand with a strong global presence, known for their ZX series of intelligent addressable fire alarm control panels. The ZX range supports Apollo and Hochiki detector protocols, making it a versatile platform for large, complex installations requiring EN 54 compliance.

CRI works with Morley-IAS as a business partner, supplying and installing ZX series panels for projects where EN 54-certified, multi-loop, networkable fire alarm systems are specified.

Key Products
