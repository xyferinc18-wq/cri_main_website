About LEELEN

Smarter entry for modern buildings.

LEELEN Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2002 in Xiamen, China, and has grown into one of Asia's most respected manufacturers of video door phone and intercom systems. Their products are installed in over 1,000 residential and commercial developments across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and Europe.

CRI is an authorized distributor and installer of LEELEN video entry systems in the Philippines, with flagship installations at Net Lima BGC, The Infinity Tower, Seda Hotels, and many more landmark properties.
