What We Deliver

Full-lifecycle fire alarm solutions.

CRI designs, supplies, installs, and maintains fire detection and alarm systems (FDAS) for high-rise residential towers, commercial malls, hotels, industrial plants, and government facilities across the Philippines.

Every system is engineered to comply with NFPA 72 and local BFP (Bureau of Fire Protection) codes. From addressable panels to conventional zones, we match the right technology to your building's risk profile and budget.

CAD-based fire alarm layout, load calculations, and panel programming.

PRC-licensed engineers on every project with full site documentation.

Testing & Certification

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled quarterly and annual inspection programs.

System Types

The right system for every building type.

Addressable Systems

Point-by-point identification of every detector and device. Ideal for large buildings, hospitals, and hotels requiring precise fault location.

Conventional Systems

Zone-based detection for smaller buildings and cost-sensitive applications. Reliable, proven technology with lower installation complexity.

Networked / Multi-Building

Linked panels across multiple buildings or floors with a central graphic annunciator — ideal for campuses, malls, and mixed-use developments.

Linear Heat Detection

UL-listed linear heat detection cable for tunnels, cable trays, conveyor systems, and areas where conventional detectors cannot be installed.
