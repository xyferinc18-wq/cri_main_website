About AMPERES

Professional audio that saves lives.

AMPERES Electronics is a Malaysia-based manufacturer of professional public address, background music, and voice evacuation systems. Their product range covers everything from small mixer amplifiers for retail stores to large-scale multi-zone PA systems for transport terminals and convention centres.

CRI is an authorized distributor of AMPERES products in the Philippines, with installations in malls, schools, government offices, and industrial facilities across the country.
