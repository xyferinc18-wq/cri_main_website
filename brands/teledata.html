About TELEDATA ONE

Italian precision in fire and security engineering.

TELEDATA ONE is an Italian manufacturer of fire detection and security systems, headquartered in Milan. Their products are designed to meet the highest European safety standards and are deployed in commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects across Europe and Asia.

CRI works with TELEDATA ONE as a business partner, providing access to Italian-engineered fire detection solutions for projects requiring EN 54 compliance and European manufacturing quality.
