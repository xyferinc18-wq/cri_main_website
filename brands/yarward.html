About YARWARD

Life-safety electronics built for reliability.

YARWARD Electronics is a Shandong, China-based manufacturer of fire detection components, interface modules, and life-safety electronic accessories. Their products provide reliable, cost-effective solutions for complementing fire alarm systems from leading panel manufacturers.

CRI works with YARWARD as a business partner, sourcing fire detection accessories and interface modules for projects where complementary components are required alongside primary fire alarm systems.
