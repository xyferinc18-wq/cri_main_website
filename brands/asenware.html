About ASENWARE

Reliable fire protection, right-sized for every project.

ASENWARE is a China-based manufacturer of fire alarm control panels, detectors, and accessories. Their product line spans conventional and addressable systems, offering reliable fire protection solutions for light commercial, residential, and mid-scale industrial projects.

CRI works with ASENWARE as a business partner for projects where cost-effective, code-compliant fire alarm solutions are required without sacrificing reliability.
