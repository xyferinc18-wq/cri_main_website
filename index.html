About the Company

Three decades of building the Philippines, system by system.

Robust Electric Distributors was established in 1988 as a single proprietorship and later incorporated into Robust Inc. — a dynamic corporation that imports and services electrical construction materials and lighting products from around the world for fast-developing markets like the Philippines.

We have been a major contributor to the success of well-known office and condominium buildings, commercial malls, and industrial plants. Reliable corporate clients including the Ayala Group, Filinvest Corporation, and leading architects and engineers trust us to deliver dependable building systems.

Today, with new investors expanding operational capacity, the company is known as Consolidated Robust Inc. (CRI) — carrying premium product lines including HOCHIKI Fire Alarm Systems, AMPERES PA/BGM, DAVIS Floor Boxes, and UL Linear Heat Detection Cable, alongside our long-running distributorships.
