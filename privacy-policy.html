Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024  ·  Last Updated: May 2025

Consolidated Robust Inc. ("CRI," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website at crobust.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us through our inquiry form.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily submit it through our contact form. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Company or organization name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Project details or inquiry message
  • The service you are interested in

We do not automatically collect personal data through cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics software beyond standard web server logs (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited). These logs are used solely for server administration and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your project inquiry or request for quotation
  • Provide you with information about our products and services
  • Follow up on ongoing project discussions

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Google reCAPTCHA

Our contact form uses Google reCAPTCHA v2 to prevent spam submissions. By submitting the form, reCAPTCHA collects hardware and software information (such as device and application data) and sends it to Google for analysis. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

4. Data Retention

Inquiry emails sent to sales@crobust.com are retained in our business email system for as long as necessary to fulfill your request and maintain business records, typically no more than 3 years unless a longer retention period is required by Philippine law or active project obligations.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for data in transit.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and are not liable for unauthorized access resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to brand partner websites (e.g., hochiki.com, asenware.com). These sites are operated by independent third parties and are governed by their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have provided to us by contacting us at the details below. We will respond to such requests within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
