What We Deliver

Systems that work when it matters most.

Life-safety systems are only as reliable as their last maintenance record. CRI's preventive maintenance program keeps your fire alarm, suppression, CCTV, and auxiliary systems in peak operating condition — with full documentation for BFP, FSIC, and insurance inspectors.

All maintenance activities are performed by PRC-licensed engineers and documented in detailed service reports, giving building administrators a clear compliance trail.

Fire Alarm System Testing

Detector sensitivity checks, panel diagnostics, and alarm circuit verification per NFPA 72.

Suppression System Inspection

Agent cylinder weight checks, valve and nozzle inspection, and discharge test readiness.

CCTV System Health Checks

Camera alignment, recording integrity, storage capacity review, and remote access verification.

Auxiliary System Audits

Intercom, access control, and PABX system functional tests and firmware updates.

Compliance Documentation

As-found and as-left service reports suitable for FSIC renewal and insurance inspections.

Service Scope

Comprehensive care for every system we install.

Fire Detection & Alarm

Detector head cleaning and sensitivity testing, loop card diagnostics, battery load testing, and full panel simulation per NFPA 72 standards.

Suppression Systems

Clean-agent and sprinkler system inspection — agent weight verification, valve operation, pressure testing, and control panel functional checks.

CCTV & Surveillance

Camera optics cleaning, field-of-view adjustment, NVR/DVR health check, recording retention verification, and remote access testing.

Annual Service Contracts

Structured quarterly or semi-annual maintenance schedules with priority response times and discounted repair rates for contract clients.
