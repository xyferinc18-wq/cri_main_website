System Types

The right suppression for every risk.

Clean-Agent Systems

Electrically non-conductive, leaves no residue — ideal for server rooms, control rooms, and archives where water damage would be catastrophic.

Linear Heat Detection Cable

UL-listed cable that activates along its entire length when heat is detected — perfect for cable trays, conveyors, and transformer bays.

Sprinkler Systems

Wet-pipe, dry-pipe, and pre-action sprinkler systems for warehouses, car parks, and general commercial occupancies.

Gaseous Suppression

CO₂ and inert gas systems for industrial equipment rooms, generator bays, and high-value storage areas.
