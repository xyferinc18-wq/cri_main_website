What We Deliver

Connected buildings from the ground up.

CRI designs and installs the complete extra-low voltage (ELV) infrastructure for modern buildings — covering everything from structured cabling and video intercom to door access control and telephone networks.

As an authorized distributor and installer of LEELEN video entry systems, CRI has deployed intercom solutions in over a dozen landmark properties including Net Lima BGC, The Infinity Tower, and Seda Hotels.
