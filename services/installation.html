What We Deliver

From drawings to commissioning — one team.

CRI manages the entire installation lifecycle: detailed shop drawing preparation, material procurement, site coordination, installation by licensed technicians, system commissioning, and handover with as-built documentation. Every project has a designated PIC (Person-In-Charge) engineer accountable for quality and schedule.

Our warehouse in Metro Manila maintains buffer stock of common materials, eliminating supply-chain delays that derail project timelines.

Shop Drawing Preparation

Detailed installation drawings, riser diagrams, and single-line diagrams for all systems.

Turn-Key Installation

Full installation by licensed electrical and electronics engineers and certified technicians.

System Commissioning

End-to-end system testing, loop addressing, panel programming, and integrated system trials.

As-Built Documentation

Complete as-built drawings, equipment schedules, and O&M manuals handed over at project close.

Defects Liability Support

Post-commissioning defects rectification and warranty-period support for all installed systems.

Our Process

Structured delivery, every step.

Design & Coordination

Shop drawing submission, material approval with the consultant, and site coordination with the general contractor before mobilization.

Site Installation

Rough-in works (conduit, cable tray, cabling), equipment mounting, panel installation, and all terminations by qualified technicians.

Testing & Commissioning

Point-by-point device testing, loop integrity checks, system integration trials, and third-party witness testing where required.

Handover & Support

As-built documentation, operator training, warranty registration, and scheduled post-handover check-ups for system reliability.
